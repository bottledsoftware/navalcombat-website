Naval Combat for iMessage brings the classic Battleships guessing game to the Messages app on your iOS device. You can play with your friends right in your Messages conversation without even leaving the app.

Features

directly integrated into the ios 10 messages app

  • All actions can be performed without even leaving the iOS 10 Messages App
  • Animated messages allow you to backtrace your opponent's turn at a glance

usability

  • Naval Combat for iMessage is optimized for all iPhones and iPads that are capable of running iOS 10
  • Naval Combat for iMessage works in portrait and landscape mode

supported languages and accessibility

  • The following languages are supported: English, German, French, Chinese (simplified), Chinese (traditional)
  • Naval Combat for iMessage supports VoiceOver
